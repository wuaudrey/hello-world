Audrey's Favorite restaurants, cafes, places, etc:

Boston's Vietnamese cafe. Phinsta is the combination of Phin (a style of brewed coffee) and well, your barista.

96 Peterborough St.
Boston, MA 02215
Dumpling Cafe

695 Washington St.
Boston, MA 02111
